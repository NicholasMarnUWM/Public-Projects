Ordered List Example

How to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwhich w/ an ordered list!
  1. Get ingredients & supplies
    1. Two slices of bread
    2. One jar of peanut butter
    3. One jar of jelly
    4. One knife and One spoon
  2. Take the two slices of bread and set them next to each other
  3. Apply peanut butter to both slices of bread that are facing upwards
  4. Apply jelly two one slice of the bread. This will serve as the bottom half of the sandwich
  5. Take the slice of bread without jelly and rotate this slice 180 degrees, placing the peanut butter side on top of the jelly
  6. Enjoy!
